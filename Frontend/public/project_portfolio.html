Deployment & Hosting

The frontend is deployed on Vercel, ensuring fast load times and seamless performance. In backend nodemailer is used for sending message. Its data is saved in MngoDB atlas. The backend is hosted on Render, ensuring a reliable and scalable server infrastructure.
